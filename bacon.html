Crispy Air Fryer Bacon

Description

This is a quick 8 minute air fryer bacon recipe with an abundance of flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 strips of bacon
  • Kinder's Buttery Poultry Seasoning

Steps

  1. Preheat AIr Fryer to 320 degrees
  2. While air fryer preheats, set bacon strips on a rack and lightly season with Kinder's
  3. Once preheat is finished, set rack with bacon in the air fryer
  4. If using frozen bacon, set to 320 degree for 8 minutes, other air fryer for 5-6 minutes
  5. ONce complete, Enjoy!
