Bacon and Scallion Omelet

Description

This is a flavorful yet healthy omelet that has bacon and onions. The onions bring out the aroma and taste. With Kinder's Garlic & Herb seasoning and soy sauce, this is a mixture of Japanese and American comfort food.

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 strips of cooked bacon
  • Olive Oil (Alternative: Avocado Oil)
  • Your choice of shredded cheese (MOst Common: MOzzarella or Cheddar)
  • Chopped Scallions/Green Onions
  • Kinder's Garlic & Herb Seasoning
  • 1/4 cup of milk

Steps

  1. Cook Bacon & Chop into bits
  2. In mixing bowl, combine eggs and milk.
  3. In pan, set to low-medium heat
  4. LIghtly spray or coat with oil
  5. ONce heated, add egg mixture to pan
  6. Once a thin layer develops, add bacon and seasoning
  7. ONce layer becomes firmly, with spatula, flip half of the side to cover the other half
  8. Once it turns golden on the underside, flip over for two minutes
  9. Done!
